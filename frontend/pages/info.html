What can you use the generated map data for?

Map data generated for training artificial intelligence (AI) can be used for a wide range of applications, significantly enhancing the capabilities of various systems and services. Here are some of the key uses:

Autonomous Vehicles: AI models trained with map data can help autonomous vehicles understand and navigate the environment, recognizing everything from road boundaries and lanes to obstacles and traffic control elements.

Geospatial Analysis: AI can analyze map data to identify patterns, predict trends, and make decisions in areas such as urban planning, environmental monitoring, disaster management, and resource allocation.

Location-Based Services: Enhancing services like navigation apps, delivery services, and ride-sharing platforms, AI can optimize routes, predict traffic conditions, and improve overall user experience.

Agriculture: AI models can use satellite imagery and map data to monitor crop health, predict yields, optimize farming practices, and enhance precision agriculture.

Environmental Monitoring: Analyzing terrain, vegetation, and changes over time, AI can contribute to monitoring deforestation, urbanization, or the effects of climate change, aiding in conservation efforts.

Urban Planning and Smart Cities: AI can help in analyzing urban growth, planning infrastructure, optimizing public transportation routes, and improving emergency response through detailed map data.

Real Estate and Property Management: AI can assess land use, property values, and market trends by analyzing geographic and environmental data from maps.

Defense and Security: For surveillance, reconnaissance, and mission planning, AI can provide detailed terrain analysis, route planning, and simulation based on comprehensive map data.

Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR): Creating immersive experiences by integrating real-world geographic data into AR and VR environments, enabling applications in gaming, education, and training simulations.

Logistics and Supply Chain Management: Optimizing delivery routes, warehouse locations, and supply chain logistics by analyzing geographic and traffic data, leading to increased efficiency and reduced operational costs.

About KartAi

A research project for improving the quality of the property register in Norway (matrikkelen) and Sentral felles kartdatabase (SFKB) using artificial intelligence (AI).

The aim is to contribute to automatization and to optimize the application process for construction applications.

A collaboration between Kristiansand municipality (project owner), Kartverket, Norkart AS and the University of Agder.

Financially supported by Regionale forskninsfond Agder.

The initiators are Alexander Salveson Nossum, Norkart AS; Terje Nuland, Kristiansand municipality; Lars Fredrik Gyland and Ivar Oveland, Kartverket.
